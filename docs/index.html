Features

What SaveMeGB does

Scans your drives for files that games left behind after uninstalling — saves you'd never find manually.

🔍

Deep Scanning

Three scan modes — Quick, Standard, and Deep — find orphans across your entire system. Choose speed or thoroughness.

🎮

9 Launcher Support

Steam, Epic Games, Xbox, GOG Galaxy, Battle.net, Riot Games, EA App, Ubisoft Connect, and manual installs. All covered.

📊

Insights & Charts

Visual breakdown of what's wasting your space — donut and bar charts by category, game, and launcher. Data at a glance.

🛡️

Safe Deletion

Three strategies: Recycle Bin (undoable), Backup Folder (safe copy), and Force Delete (for locked files). You're in control.

📄

Export Reports

Export scan results as JSON or CSV. Keep records, share with friends, or analyze in your favorite spreadsheet.

⭐

Whitelist Protection

Never accidentally delete something you care about. Whitelist paths and game names — they'll be skipped forever.

How It Works

Three steps to a cleaner drive

Download, scan, clean. No sign-up. No bloat. Just reclaimed gigabytes.

1

Download & Install

Lightweight installer (~5 MB). Runs on Windows 10/11. No admin required for scanning.

2

Scan Your System

Pick Quick, Standard, or Deep mode. SaveMeGB crawls launcher folders, AppData, and Documents.

3

Clean Up Orphans

Review results, select what to delete, and reclaim your space. Items go to the Recycle Bin by default.
